# HAVEN – Services Page UX & Content Spec

This document defines the UX, layout, and copy direction for `services.html`, turning it into a premium “Our Expertise” page that clearly explains how HAVEN helps buyers, sellers, investors, landlords, and international clients. [web:5][web:31][web:47]

---

## 1. Page Hero & Intro

**Current:**

- Eyebrow: `OUR EXPERTISE`
- H1: `PremiumServices`
- Subheading: `End-to-end property solutions for discerning buyers and sellers.`

**Issues:**

- Missing space in `PremiumServices`.
- Hero is minimal; doesn’t yet mirror the cinematic, lifestyle‑driven hero feel used on top luxury real‑estate sites. [web:5][web:47]

**Proposed structure:**

- Eyebrow: `OUR EXPERTISE`
- H1: `Premium Services`
- Subheading:  
  `End-to-end property solutions for discerning buyers, sellers, investors, and international clients.`

**Design notes:**

- Reuse the homepage hero typography (same H1 size, weight, letter spacing) and spacing. [web:31][web:47]
- Use a refined background: either a subtle image illustrating a meeting/consultation, or a toned architectural shot, with soft overlay for contrast.
- Add a primary CTA above the fold: `Speak with an advisor` (scrolls to contact/consult form) to follow good landing‑page practice. [web:46][web:49]

## 2. Services Overview Block

Turn the list of services into an at‑a‑glance “grid” so visitors instantly see everything you offer. [web:31][web:47]

**Current services:**

- Buyer’s Advisory
- Property Appraisal
- Property Management
- Investment Advisory
- International Buyers
- Conveyancing

**Recommended layout:**

- 2×3 or 3×2 card grid with icon + title + short description for each service.
- Each card can link to an anchor section lower on this page for more detail, or to a dedicated future service page.

**Example card content:**

- **Buyer’s Advisory**  
  `Independent guidance across the entire buying journey – from search and shortlisting to negotiation and settlement.`

- **Property Appraisal**  
  `Detailed market appraisals to help you price with confidence and prepare a strategy for sale.`

- **Property Management**  
  `Full-service management for prestige rentals, ensuring your property is protected and your tenants are cared for.`

- **Investment Advisory**  
  `Portfolio‑level advice on acquisitions, divestments, and long‑term performance across key Australian markets.`

- **International Buyers**  
  `Specialised support for overseas investors and expats navigating local regulations, finance, and settlement.`

- **Conveyancing**  
  `Seamless coordination with trusted legal partners to manage contracts, searches, and settlement.`

Design notes:

- Use the same card style (radius, shadow, spacing) as you use for “Why Haven” or features on the homepage. [web:5][web:31]
- Keep each description 1–2 lines for scannability. [web:46]

## 3. Detailed Service Sections

Below the overview grid, add deeper sections for each key service. Each section should have: title, short intro, bullets (what’s included), and a focused CTA. [web:46][web:49]

### 3.1 Buyer’s Advisory

**Current:**  
`Expert guidance through every step of your property purchase journey.`

**Expanded copy:**

Short intro:  
`From first brief to key handover, our buyer’s advisory team acts exclusively in your interests.`

Bullets (what’s included):

- Define your brief and shortlist suitable suburbs and property types.
- Provide on‑ and off‑market opportunities via our network.
- Conduct inspections and provide independent value assessments.
- Lead negotiations and bidding strategies at auction or private treaty.
- Coordinate due diligence, building and pest, and legal reviews.

CTA: `Start your buying brief`

## 7. Design System Consistency

To keep `services.html` aligned with your other pages: [web:5][web:31][web:47]

- **Typography**
  - Same H1/H2 scale as Home/Properties.
  - Service titles use consistent heading level (e.g. H3) and size.

- **Colour & Imagery**
  - Reuse brand accent colour for icons, bullets, and CTAs.
  - Use a consistent photo style (colour grading, composition) if you add imagery for services.

- **Layout & Spacing**
  - Same section padding and grid gutters across all pages.
  - Use uniform card radius/shadow for service cards.
